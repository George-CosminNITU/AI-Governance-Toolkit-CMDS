## 2. State of the Art (SotA) Summary
### Regulatory Landscape
* [cite_start]**EU AI Act:** Imposes strict transparency and governance obligations; non-compliance results in heavy fines[cite: 10, 11].
* [cite_start]**GDPR:** Standard AI contracts often conflict with GDPR, requiring clear protocols for data sovereignty[cite: 12, 13].
* [cite_start]**NIST AI RMF:** Used as a foundational standard for framework alignment[cite: 34, 41].

### Technical Analysis of AI Providers
* [cite_start]**Data Retention:** Analyzing how OpenAI, Microsoft, and Google handle corporate inputs and model training[cite: 16, 33].
* [cite_start]**Current Gap:** Organizations currently rely on total bans or have no policy, lacking simple operational checklists for IT Managers[cite: 21, 24].
